Values of these properties are technically numbers, but these numbers 
are “of pixels”, so these are pixel measurements.

1)offsetParent
2)offsetLeft
3)offsetTop

4)offsetWidth - content + padding + scroll bar + border. its zero for inline elements and elements with 
    no css.
5)offsetHeight- content + padding + scroll bar + border

6)clientLeft - left boerder width + scroll bar(if text is right to left)
7)clientTop - top border width

8)clientWidth -  content-width + padding (not scroll bar)
9)clientHeight - content-height + padding(not scroll bar, not border)

10)scrollWidth - height that element will take if we have no scroll bar.
11)scrollHeight - height that element will take if we do not have scroll.(Full inner width)

12)scrollTop/scrollLeft - Properties scrollLeft/scrollTop are the width/height of the hidden, 
    scrolled out part of the element.Most of the geometry properties here are read-only, 
    but scrollLeft/scrollTop can be changed, and the browser will scroll the element.
